Asymptote integrates into four stages of your [Software Development Lifecycle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Systems_development_life_cycle) (SDLC) to catch vulnerabilities before they reach production: code generation, CI/pull requests, pre-production testing, and asynchronous codebase scans.

| Stage | When | What Asymptote Does |
|-------|------|---------------------|
| [**Code Generation**](/ai-tools/code-generation) | As code is written in your IDE | Scans diffs in real time, flags risky patterns, and enforces policies on every AI tool call |
| [**CI / Pull Requests**](/ai-tools/ci-time) | When a PR is opened or updated | Reviews the full diff, posts inline findings, and blocks merge on policy violations |
| [**Pre-production Testing**](/ai-tools/pre-production) | Before deploying to staging or production | Whitebox agentic pentesting |
| [**Asynchronous Codebase Scans**](/ai-tools/async-scans) | On demand or on a schedule | Full policy sweep across your entire codebase, independent of your deployment pipeline |
