## Overview

Even with [guardrails](/features/guardrails) at code generation and CI time, pre-production testing provides a final security gate before changes reach your live environment. Asymptote uses whitebox agentic [penetration testing](/introduction/core-concepts#penetration-test) to actively probe your codebase for exploitable [vulnerabilities](/introduction/core-concepts#vulnerability), going beyond [static analysis](/introduction/core-concepts#static-analysis) to simulate how an attacker would reason about and exploit weaknesses.

## Whitebox Agentic Pentesting

Asymptote's agentic pentesting has full access to your source code and reasons across the entire codebase to find vulnerabilities that diff-level or file-level tools miss.

- **Exploitability-focused:** prioritizes findings that are actually reachable and exploitable, not just theoretical
- **Full codebase context:** traces data flows, identifies trust boundaries, and finds chained vulnerabilities
- **AI-driven reasoning:** the agent reasons across your entire codebase, not just individual files or diffs

## Audit and Reporting

Every pre-production scan produces a timestamped security report that includes:

- Which policies were evaluated
- Which findings were detected, suppressed, or resolved
- The identity of the agent or developer responsible for each change
- A full audit trail suitable for compliance review
