## Overview

Invite developers, security engineers, and team leads to your Asymptote organization so everyone has visibility into security findings and policy enforcement.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Organization Management">
    From the dashboard, click your organization name in the top navigation bar and select **Manage** from the dropdown.

    <img src="/images/org-management.png" alt="Organization management dropdown" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Members Page">
    In the organization settings panel, select **Members** from the left sidebar.

    <img src="/images/org-settings.png" alt="Organization settings" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite a Member">
    Under the **Members** tab, click **Invite** and enter the email address of the person you'd like to add.

    <img src="/images/member-settings.png" alt="Members settings" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a Role">
    Select the appropriate role for the new member:

    | Role | Access |
    |------|--------|
    | **Admin** | Full access including organization settings and team management |
    | **Member** | Access to repositories, findings, and policy packs |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the Invitation">
    Click **Invite**. The recipient will receive an email with a link to join your organization. You can track pending invites under the **Invitations** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Team Members

From **Organization → Members** you can:

- **View pending invitations** under the Invitations tab
- **Change a member's role** at any time using the role dropdown next to their name
- **Remove members** via the actions menu on their row
