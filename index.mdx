## Welcome to Asymptote

Asymptote helps teams ship secure, compliant software by preventing vulnerabilities at the source. Our platform enforces security at every stage of the SDLC: scanning code in real time as it's written, reviewing pull requests and blocking on policy violations, and running agentic pentests before you ship. Our AI observability platform gives security teams the visibility and control they need into code vulnerability management and AI tool usage.
