The Slack integration lets Asymptote post notifications directly to a Slack channel. Once connected, you can receive alerts when security scans complete so your team stays informed without having to check the dashboard.

You can configure the integration from **Integrations** in the dashboard sidebar under **Admin**.

## Connecting Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an incoming webhook in Slack">
    In Slack, go to **Apps** and search for **Incoming Webhooks**. Select the channel you want Asymptote to post to and click **Add Incoming Webhooks Integration**. Copy the webhook URL that is generated.

    You can also follow the in-app link **How to create a Slack webhook** on the Integrations page for step-by-step guidance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the webhook URL">
    In the Asymptote dashboard, open **Integrations** and paste your webhook URL into the **Webhook URL** field under Slack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure alerts">
    Enable **Scan completion alerts** to receive a notification each time a security scan finishes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and test">
    Click **Save & Test**. Asymptote will send a test message to your Slack channel to confirm the connection is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Disconnecting Slack

To remove the integration, click **Disconnect** on the Integrations page. This immediately stops all notifications and removes the saved webhook URL.

## Access

The Integrations page is restricted to organization admins.
